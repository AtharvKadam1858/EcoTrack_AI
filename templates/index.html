Quick Tips

AI-Curated Sustainability Tips

⚡ Energy

Switch to LED lighting. LEDs consume 75% less energy than traditional bulbs and last 25× longer — easiest ROI in any office.

💰 Save ₹8,000–₹15,000/year
💧 Water

Install motion-sensor faucets in washrooms. Automatic shut-off prevents water running when not in use — saves 40% water instantly.

💰 Save ₹3,000–₹6,000/year
📄 Paper

Implement digital-first policy — all internal documents shared digitally, double-sided printing mandatory for necessary prints.

💰 Save ₹5,000–₹12,000/year
⚡ Energy

Set computers to sleep after 5 minutes of inactivity. Enable power management company-wide across all devices and monitors.

💰 Save ₹12,000–₹20,000/year
🌿 Carbon

Quarterly energy audits identify high-consumption equipment. Replace aging ACs and servers with 5-star BEE rated alternatives.

💰 Save ₹25,000–₹50,000/year
💧 Water

Rainwater harvesting for toilet flushing and irrigation. A 500 sq ft roof collects 150,000+ litres annually in Pune/Mumbai.

💰 Save ₹8,000–₹18,000/year
About Us

EcoTrack AI was built during Hack For Green Bharat 2026 — a hackathon dedicated to building technology for environmental impact.

🎯
Our Mission

The climate crisis cannot be solved by governments alone. Every office, every building, every organization must take measurable, data-driven action. EcoTrack AI gives them the tools to predict carbon footprint, track resource usage, and take AI-guided steps toward Net Zero — making carbon intelligence accessible to every Indian office regardless of size or budget.
